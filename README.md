# Phishing Detection & Awareness Report

**Analyst:** Aarif Ahmad  
**Date:** May 2026  

## 🎯 Objective
To educate employees and business owners on how to identify phishing emails, protect company data, and build a human firewall. This report analyses real‑world samples, classifies risks, and provides clear prevention guidelines.

## 🔒 Scope & Ethics
- **Analysis type:** Passive, read‑only.  
- **Emails examined:** 3 samples (2 phishing, 1 safe).  
- **Tools used:** Google Messageheader, MXToolbox Email Header Analyzer, browser hover inspection.  
- **No interaction with attacker infrastructure** — all links and attachments were examined without execution.

## 📁 Repository Contents
- `report/Phishing_Awareness_Report.pdf` – Full professional report.
- `evidence/` – Raw email samples, header analysis exports, and annotated screenshots.
- `README.md` – This file.

## 🔍 Key Findings
| Sample | Subject                        | Risk       |
|--------|--------------------------------|------------|
| 1      | Urgent: Account Will Be Locked | Phishing   |
| 2      | Invoice Attached               | Phishing   |
| 3      | Monthly HR Newsletter          | Safe       |

All red flags are explained in simple language, and the report includes a ready‑to‑use **S.T.O.P.** rule for employee training.

## 🛡️ Prevention Highlights
- Hover over links before clicking.
- Verify sender domains.
- Never open unexpected attachments.
- Report suspicious emails immediately.
